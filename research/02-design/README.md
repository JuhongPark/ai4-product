# AI for Product Design & Prototyping

## Summary

Application of generative AI to product design, UX, and prototyping — covering design automation, adaptive interfaces, and trust in AI-designed experiences.

## Sub-topics

- **Generative AI in Design** — ML, DL, NLP, computer vision applied to product design
- **AI-Driven UX & Adaptive Interfaces** — Generative UI, dynamic interfaces based on user intent and context
- **Trust in AI-Designed Experiences** — Transparency, control, consistency, and support when systems fail

## Key Stats

- 73% of designers say AI as a design collaborator will have the most impact in 2026
- 54% of designers report clients want to adopt AI trends without clear use cases (biggest gap)
- By 2026, 80%+ organizations will use generative AI

## Research Gaps

- Limited academic research on AI-generated UI evaluation methodologies
- No frameworks for measuring AI design quality vs. human design quality
- Generative UI is mostly industry-driven; minimal peer-reviewed research
