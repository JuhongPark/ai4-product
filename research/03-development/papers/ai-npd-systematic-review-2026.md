# AI in NPD: Systematic Literature Reviews (2026 Update)

## Key Paper 1

**"The use of artificial intelligence in new product development: A systematic literature review, conceptual framework, and future research agenda"**
— Vallé, Seitz, Kanbach & Schuhmacher, Technovation, Vol. 153, May 2026
- Source: https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/S0166497226000763

### Summary
Systematic review of **98 peer-reviewed articles** examining AI's role in NPD. Grounded in organizational information-processing theory.

### Conceptual Framework (5 Dimensions)
1. **Information-processing capacities** through AI in NPD
2. **Application of AI** in the NPD process
3. **Benefits of AI** in NPD
4. **Enablers of AI** in NPD
5. **Challenges of AI** in NPD

### Key Findings
- AI effects are **stage-dependent and cumulative**: early ideation quality links to downstream development efficiency and post-launch learning
- AI reconfigures information-processing and decision-making throughout product development
- Critical boundary conditions: data availability, governance, and organizational readiness
- As NPD moves from exploration to execution, AI reduces interpretive ambiguity and coordination effort

## Key Paper 2

**"Moneyball for New Product Development"**
— Robert G. Cooper, McMaster University, February 2026
- Source: https://www.academia.edu/165246907/Moneyball_for_New_Product_Development_Feb_2026_Cooper

### AI-PRISM Model
- Data-driven "Moneyball" approach to predict new product success
- AI-developed scoring model built from **7 weighted success factors and 20 sub-factors**
- Source Quality Rating Framework that weights information by credibility
- Simulated multiple runs to approximate how expert panels might score evidence

### The Problem It Solves
- ~80% of NPD investment decisions prove wrong: many approved projects fail commercially or are later canceled after substantial investment
- Traditional intuition-driven go/kill decisions are deeply flawed

### Role in Decision-Making
- Practical middle-path decision support tool (not autonomous decision-maker)
- Rigorous reality check for project teams at gate meetings
- Feeds improved probabilities into Expected Commercial Value calculations
- Now through beta tests, validated and ready for public use

## Key Paper 3

**"Integrating AI into the Front End of New Product Development"**
— Research-Technology Management, Vol. 68, No. 2, 2024
- Source: https://www.tandfonline.com/doi/full/10.1080/08956308.2024.2444142

### Summary
Demonstrates how large-scale customer data can be synthesized to enhance decision-making, accelerate concept development, and improve product evaluation efficiency in the fuzzy front end (FFE) of NPD.

- Cooper's AI-PRISM addresses the critical gap in data-driven NPD decision support
- Together, these papers significantly advance understanding of AI across all NPD phases
