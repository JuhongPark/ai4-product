# AI for Product Development & Engineering

## Summary

AI application across the new product development (NPD) process — from systematic literature reviews mapping AI methods to NPD phases, to empirical evidence on firm growth impact.

## Sub-topics

- **AI in NPD** — Systematic review of 190 publications; AI method mapping across NPD phases
- **AI Impact on Firm Growth** — Strong association between AI adoption and firm growth via product innovation
- **Practical Applications** — Time-to-market reduction, cost savings, accelerated physical product innovation

## Key Stats

- Early adopters: up to 50% reduction in NPD time
- Companies using AI: 20-40% time-to-market reduction, 20-30% cost reduction
- Later stages (testing, validation, post-launch optimization) significantly under-researched

## Research Gaps

- AI application in concept testing stage is significantly under-researched
- No integrative AI solutions spanning multiple NPD phases end-to-end
- Absence of systematic frameworks for AI-driven product management
- Limited empirical studies on AI's impact on software product development quality
