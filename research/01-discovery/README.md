# AI for Product Discovery & User Research

## Summary

Application of AI across product discovery and user research — from automated UX evaluation to LLM-powered customer insights and synthetic research methods.

## Sub-topics

- **AI-Automated User Research** — Transition from rule-based to learning-based approaches; sharp rise in LLM/GenAI usage since 2022
- **LLM for Customer Insights** — LLM-based market research, consumer segmentation, review analysis, automated insight generation
- **Synthetic User Research** — AI persona interviews, synthetic research methodology
- **AI for Product Discovery** — Scientific and commercial product discovery via GenAI

## Key Stats

- 80% of UX researchers now use AI (2025, +24pt YoY)
- AI-assisted researchers discover 44% more materials -> 39% more patents -> 17% more product innovation
- 58% of Gen Z shoppers already use AI when shopping

## Research Gaps

- Limited validation of LLM-generated insights against ground truth customer data
- Ethical concerns around synthetic user research replacing real user voices
- Lack of standardized evaluation metrics for AI-generated customer insights
