# AI for Product Growth & Lifecycle Management

## Summary

AI applications in product lifecycle management (PLM), digital twins, personalization, and growth strategy.

## Sub-topics

- **PLM** — AI-driven process automation and decision support analytics
- **Digital Twins + Generative AI** — Simulation-driven design, reduced prototyping costs
- **AI for Personalization & Growth** — Recommendation engines, A/B test automation, real-time personalization at scale
- **Industry Case Studies** — Mondelez, BMW, Netflix, PepsiCo, Grammarly

## Key Stats

- Global PLM market: $53.9B (2023), CAGR 12.6%
- Mondelez: 4-5x faster recipe development with AI
- BMW: >$1M annual savings from AI quality control

## Research Gaps

- Limited research on AI for post-launch product optimization
- Digital twin applications mostly in manufacturing; under-explored in software products
- AI experimentation platform research is mostly industry-driven, not academic
