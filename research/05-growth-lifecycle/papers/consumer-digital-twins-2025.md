# Consumer Digital Twins & AI-Driven Product Lifecycle (2025-2026 Update)

## Key Paper

**"AI-enabled consumer digital twins as a platform for research aimed at enhancing customer experience"**
— Management Review Quarterly (Springer), 2025
- Source: https://link.springer.com/article/10.1007/s11301-025-00527-3

## Summary

Proposes AI-enabled consumer digital twins as a research platform for understanding and enhancing customer experience. Extends the digital twin concept from manufacturing/engineering into consumer research and CX domains.
