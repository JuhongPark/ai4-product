# MIT Research on AI for Product

MIT conducts leading research on AI applications across the product lifecycle through multiple labs and departments. This section organizes MIT's key research institutions and their AI for Product contributions.

## Key Research Institutions

| Institution | Affiliation | Core Research Areas |
|-------------|-------------|---------------------|
| **MIT Sloan Management Review (SMR)** | MIT Sloan | AI Strategy, Product Development, Responsible AI |
| **MIT Initiative on the Digital Economy (IDE)** | MIT Sloan | AI Productivity, Digital Experimentation, Agentic AI |
| **MIT CSAIL** | EECS | AI for Software Engineering, HCI, Generative AI |
| **MIT Media Lab** | Architecture + Planning | Human-AI Collaboration, Design Tools, Interaction |
| **MIT Center for Collective Intelligence (CCI)** | MIT Sloan | Collective Intelligence, Human-AI Teamwork |
| **DeCoDE Lab** | Mechanical Engineering | AI-Powered Engineering Design |
| **MIT Design Intelligence Lab** | Architecture | LLM Interfaces, Generative Design |
| **MIT Senseable City Lab** | DUSP | Data-Driven Product Innovation |
| **MIT CISR** | MIT Sloan | Enterprise AI Maturity |
| **MIT SDM** | Engineering + Sloan | System Architecture, AI Product Design, Multi-Agent Systems |
| **MIT LIDS** | EECS | AI Decision-Making, Optimization under Uncertainty |
