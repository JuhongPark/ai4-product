# AI for Product Management & Decision Making

## Summary

Current state and outlook of AI-powered PM tools, agentic AI, and automated decision-making in product management.

## Sub-topics

- **AI-Powered PM Tools** — Analysis of 51 tools; only ~25% have meaningful agentic capabilities
- **Key Capabilities** — Real-time predictive insights, unstructured data analysis, automated prioritization, autonomous decision-making
- **Agentic AI in Product Workflows** — Task-specific AI agents, low-code/no-code deployment
- **Industry Perspectives** — HBR, McKinsey viewpoints

## Key Stats

- Only ~25% of AI PM tools have meaningful agentic capabilities
- Gartner: 15% of daily work decisions will be made by agentic AI by 2028
- 40% of enterprise apps will feature AI agents by end of 2026 (up from <5% in 2025)
- AI agent market: $7.84B (2025) -> $52.62B (2030), CAGR 46.3%

## Research Gaps

- Agentic PM capabilities are least mature and under-studied
- 39.7% of product professionals cite AI-generated insight quality as biggest concern
- Limited academic research on AI-augmented product decision-making effectiveness
- No standardized benchmarks for measuring AI PM tool performance
