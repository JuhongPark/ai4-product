# Agentic AI in Product Management: Co-Evolutionary Model

## Key Paper

**"Agentic AI in Product Management: A Co-Evolutionary Model"**
— Nishant A. Parikh, arXiv:2507.01069, 2025
- Source: https://arxiv.org/abs/2507.01069

## Core Argument

Agentic AI — characterized by autonomy, goal-oriented decision-making, and multi-agent collaboration — transforms product management roles. Proposes a co-evolutionary framework to guide integration across the product lifecycle.

## Key Contributions

### 1. Co-Evolutionary Framework
Maps agentic AI capabilities across all PM phases:
- Product discovery
- Scoping
- Business case development
- Development
- Testing
- Launch

### 2. Role Redefinition
Product managers repositioned as **"orchestrators of socio-technical ecosystems"** rather than traditional managers. Emphasis shifts to:
- AI orchestration and supervision
- Strategic alignment
- System-level thinking

### 3. Theoretical Foundation
Integrates three theories:
- **Systems theory** — understanding AI as part of complex product ecosystems
- **Co-evolutionary theory** — mutual adaptation between PMs and AI systems
- **Human-AI interaction theory** — designing effective collaboration patterns

### 4. Essential Competencies for Modern PMs
- AI literacy
- Governance expertise
- Systems thinking

### 5. Evidence Base
Synthesizes **70+ sources** including case studies from leading technology companies.
