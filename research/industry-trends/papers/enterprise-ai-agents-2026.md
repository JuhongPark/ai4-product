## Critical Success Factors

1. **Domain specificity**: Successful deployments focus on well-defined domains, not enterprise-wide automation
2. **Governance**: 50% of agent deployment failures by 2030 will be due to insufficient governance (Gartner)
3. **Orchestration**: Multiple specialized agents working in coordinated fashion outperform monolithic systems
4. **Human-in-the-loop**: Hybrid approaches with human oversight continue to outperform fully autonomous systems
