## Key Findings

1. **AI Value Gap**: 88% adoption but only 6% are high performers (McKinsey); 74% want revenue growth from AI but only 20% have achieved it (Deloitte)
2. **Copilot-to-Agent Transition**: Every major platform pivoting from assistive copilots to autonomous agentic AI (2025-2026)
3. **Vibe Coding Explosion**: AI dev platforms (Cursor $29.3B, Lovable $6.6B, Replit $9B) collectively >$45B in valuations
4. **Process > Technology**: 25-30% productivity gains require end-to-end workflow transformation vs. 10-15% from tool adoption alone (Bain)
5. **Speed-to-Market Compression**: PepsiCo 6 weeks (vs. 6-9 months), Mondelez 4-5x faster, P&G 22% reduction, Deloitte reports 20-40% time-to-market reduction
6. **Multi-Agent Breakthrough**: 2026 is the breakthrough year for multi-agent systems (Forrester + Gartner consensus)
7. **Enterprise Adoption Reality**: 30% exploring, 38% piloting, 14% ready, only 11% in production (March 2026)
8. **AI Agent Economic Impact**: McKinsey projects $2.6-4.4T annually; Gartner projects 30% of enterprise app revenue by 2035
