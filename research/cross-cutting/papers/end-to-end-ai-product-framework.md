# End-to-End AI Product Lifecycle Frameworks

> Addressing the highest-severity research gap: No integrative AI framework spanning all product lifecycle phases

## Key Sources

### 1. McKinsey: AI-Enabled Software PDLC
**"How an AI-enabled software product development life cycle will fuel innovation"**
— McKinsey Technology, 2025
- Source: https://www.mckinsey.com/industries/technology-media-and-telecommunications/our-insights/how-an-ai-enabled-software-product-development-life-cycle-will-fuel-innovation

Proposes integrating AI across the entire software PDLC to empower PMs, engineers, and teams to spend more time on higher-value work. Emphasizes that end-to-end integration yields greater benefits than point solutions.

### 2. EY.ai PDLC (March 2026)
**"Ernst & Young LLP and 8090 launch EY.ai PDLC"**
— EY Newsroom, March 2026
- Source: https://www.ey.com/en_us/newsroom/2026/03/ernst-young-llp-and-8090-launch-ey-ai-pdlc

**Key Innovation:**
- AI-native approach replacing "outdated, linear software development"
- Orchestrates a **"collaborative mesh of AI agents"** with human oversight across the full lifecycle
- Covers: requirements → architecture → code → testing → infrastructure → operations
- Claims: 80x delivery acceleration, 70% productivity improvement, 95%+ automated test coverage
- Technology: 8090's Software Factory platform with multiple AI model orchestration

## Framework Comparison

| Framework | Scope | Approach | AI Integration |
|-----------|-------|----------|----------------|
| McKinsey PDLC | Software Products | Strategic | Point-to-full lifecycle |
| EY.ai PDLC | Enterprise Software | AI-Native | Multi-agent mesh |
| AWS AI-SDLC | Software Engineering | DevOps-Integrated | Requirements-to-deployment |
| Microsoft AI-SDLC | Software Development | Agentic | Azure + GitHub ecosystem |
| Vallé et al. (2026) | NPD (All Products) | Academic Framework | 5-dimension conceptual model |

## Key Characteristics of 2026 End-to-End Frameworks

1. **Iterative, not linear**: Continuous feedback loops replacing sequential phases
2. **Multi-agent orchestration**: Multiple specialized AI agents coordinated under human oversight
3. **Real-time data integration**: Predictive analytics and automation at every stage
4. **Human-in-the-loop**: All frameworks emphasize human oversight and strategic direction
5. **Continuous improvement**: Post-deployment learning feeds back into development

## Research Gaps Remaining

- **Academic Framework Gap**: Most end-to-end frameworks are industry-driven; Vallé et al. (2026) is the closest academic contribution
- **Cross-Domain Integration**: Frameworks are software-centric; physical product lifecycle integration largely absent
- **Empirical Validation**: No large-scale empirical studies comparing end-to-end AI frameworks against traditional methods
- **SME Applicability**: Frameworks designed for large enterprises; unclear applicability to smaller organizations
- **Measurement**: No standardized metrics for evaluating end-to-end AI product lifecycle effectiveness
